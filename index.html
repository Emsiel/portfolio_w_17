About

Hi I'm Emma

Welcome to my corner of the digital world, where I hope to take a deep dive into new and old passions through UX/UI design.

I am curious about how design intersects life.

As a child I spent each birthday visiting the Royal Academy of Arts in London where I would watch the newest artistic talents make an impact on the art world. I studied Architecture and Art History as I had a need to understand design choices and why things ‘worked’.

I am a lifelong learner.

As an international educator for 10 years, I learned the importance of making things simple, visual and, always, accessible.

My background in early childhood development gives me insight into how habits form, tasks are completed and lessons are learned. I have worked in 3 continents and gained rich experience from the colleagues I collaborate with. Teaching students from over 20 countries, my curriculum design was always a working canvas; making in the moment iterations to suit my students needs.

I embrace another’s world view.

As an immigrant, expat and traveller, I learned to always ask questions, be open to new experiences and to embrace culture.

Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to sharing this creative adventure with you!
